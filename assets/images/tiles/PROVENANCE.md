## `grass_blob47.png` — 47-blob quarter-tile sheet (procedural)

- **Origin:** 100% procedurally generated by `scripts/gen-tile-blob47.mjs`
  (deterministic; run `node scripts/gen-tile-blob47.mjs` to reproduce byte-for-byte).
- **No third-party assets:** every pixel is computed from flat palette fills
  plus a distance-transform rim/rounded-corner shaper. No AI image generation,
  no scraped or copied art.
- **Technique reference (method only, no pixels):** the *approach* — flat base
  fill, cool rim on boundary edges, rounded convex / notched concave corners —
  was informed by SLYNYRD's "PixelBlog #20 — Top Down Tiles"
  (https://www.slynyrd.com/blog/2019/8/27/pixelblog-20-top-down-tiles). Only
  the general method (an uncopyrightable idea) was referenced; no Slynyrd
  artwork, palette, or pixels are used or reproduced.
- **Palette:** original, defined in the generator (base `#7cb04a`, fleck
  `#8bbe58`, rim `#4a764e`).
